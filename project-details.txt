Project Details

Find Currency API:
- provides live data 3 times a day (i.e. 15 min delay)
- tracking dollar/rand , aud/rand , pound/rand & euro/rand

Compare the data to certain levels:
- if the currency dips below a certain level an email notification should be sent
- if the currency rises above a certain level a email notification should be sent
- Send a weekly report of the currencies
